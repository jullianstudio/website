Services

  • Voiceover in native French, also in English, Spanish, and Chinese, with a French or Belgian accent.
  • Voice synchronization with your video (no lip sync) based on a reference video or timecode.
  • Writing advertising scripts, slogans, presentations for your company and your project.
  • Correction and proofreading of scripts.
  • Translation of scripts into French from English and Spanish.
  • Composition and production of original music: background music or larger-scale composition projects.
  • Soundtrack production for your videos: music, voiceover, sound effects, mixing, mastering.

Included:
A standard voiceover service includes studio fees, recording, cleaning, and basic editing of takes (derushing, removing noisy breaths), quality control (listening review), usage rights within the specified scope, exporting of 10 individual files, and 1 revision (2 for short content).

Not included:
This does not include script changes that occur after the recording has started. Such changes will be charged (minimum of 85€). Any errors on my part will, of course, be re-recorded free of charge.

Price

The elements that determine the price of a voiceover service and rights buyout are:

  • The type of usage: advertising, corporate, e-learning, mobile application, presentation, basic web use, internal use, private use (birthday, gift), etc.
  • The length of the script: either the word count or the duration of the video.
  • The distribution channel: TV, radio, web, mobile application, cinema, trade fair, documentary, etc.
  • The deadline: today, in a few days
  • For advertising: the geographic area (national, regional...), duration of the campaign, and distribution channel (web campaign, TV or radio commercial, trade fair, cinema, etc.).
