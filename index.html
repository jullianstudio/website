My voice

As a 42-year-old male voice actor, I provide French native voice over for your professional projects.

My voice is masculine, deep, warm and velvety, able to convey various emotions: mellow and relaxing, wild and husky, confident and convincing, dynamic and enthusiastic, professional and informative, catchy or sensual… in order to match your specific needs.

I've been working full-time as a voice actor over the last 9 years. Native French speaker from Belgium, with an international background, I'm completely fluent in English and Spanish, I can also speak Chinese. I'll make your content shine with a slight French accent.

As a singer-songwriter and music producer since 1998, I have a large experience in audio work, an artistic sensitivity allied with high technical skills. My voice and studio are available for you now.
